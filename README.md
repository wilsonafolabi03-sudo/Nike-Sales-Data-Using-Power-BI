# Nike-Sales-Data-Using-Power-BI

## Project Overview
This project is a comprehensive Power BI Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard built using Nike retail sales data in the United States. The dashboard answers over 20 business intelligence questions through interactive visualizations, helping stakeholders understand sales performance, product trends, regional performance, retailer contributions, and customer purchasing patterns.

## Business Objectives
### The dashboard was designed to:
Analyze overall sales performance.
Identify top-performing products.
Track units sold across regions and retailers.
Compare sales trends over time.
Evaluate regional market performance.
Understand retailer contributions to total revenue.
Monitor pricing patterns across products.
Support data-driven business decisions.

## Project Outcome
This project demonstrates the use of Power BI to convert retail sales data into actionable business insights. Through interactive dashboards and analytical visualizations, decision-makers can easily monitor sales performance, identify growth opportunities, and improve operational efficiency.
